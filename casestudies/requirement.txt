Case Studies

Case studies would typically be represented by images that illustrate success, analysis, or study. For these, abstract graphics might include:

1. Before and After Scenarios - Split graphics showing transformation.
2. Growth Metrics - Rising arrows, bar graphs.
3. Problem-Solving - Light bulbs, puzzle pieces.
4. Customer Testimonials - Quote bubbles, star ratings.
5. Business Challenges - Obstacle graphics, walls being climbed.
6. Strategy and Implementation - Chess pieces, roadmaps.
7. Financial Analysis - Dollar signs, calculators.
8. Market Research - Magnifying glasses over graphs and charts.
9. Success Stories - Trophy or medal imagery.
10. Learning and Development - Open books, graduation caps.
